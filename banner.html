About the program

PepsiCo India brings “PepSheCo Sales Stars”, a first-of-its-kind Campus Competition specially crafted for female undergraduate students of the country, who are keen to explore and pursue a career in FMCG Sales.

Through this program, all candidates will get exposure to the Corporate world while pursuing their Graduation, and the shortlisted ones will get to interact with the PepsiCo Leadership team to understand what it takes to build a successful career in Sales.
